SUTL Innovation Grant Overview

Learn about the City's $1 million grant program catalyzing innovation in the startup community. Innovation grants build pipelines to emerging industries, expand awareness of Sacramento's startup and entrepreneurial ecosystem, and help high-growth scale-ups locate and grow in Sacramento.

Support Organizations

Ecosystem Building Grants

Ecosystem Building Grants are designed to build workforce pipelines to the IoT, cybersecurity, mobility, health IT, and life science industries for startups and individuals in Sacramento. Grants will go toward establishing or expanding programmingto build or strenthen these pipelines.

SUTL Ecosystem Marketing

SUTL Ecosystem Marketing consists of three discrete solicitations that focus on promoting Sacramento's startup ecosystem along with their respective scopes of work: SUTL Grant Program Documentation (RFP), National Marketing Strategy for Scale-Up Competition (RFP), Co-Working Marketing Grants.
