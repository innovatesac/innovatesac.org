Meet the Team

The City's Innovation Team grew out of the Mayor's Office for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (MOFIE), which was formed in 2016 to adopt innovative best learnings from around the country and to serve as a liaison between City Hall, the Mayor's Office, and Sacramento's innovation community. To better align City resources, the Innovation Team joined the City Manager's Office of Innovation and Economic Development (OIED) in Spring 2018.

Aubree Taylor

Innovation Program Technician

Aubree Taylor is an Administrative Technician with the City of Sacramento’s Office of Innovation and Economic Development. She manages the City’s SUTL Innovation Grant Program in addition to other initiatives. Aubree has been with the City of Sacramento since 2014 and got her start in Sacramento’s Mayor’s Office in Constituent Affairs. She holds a BS in Materials Science & Engineering from the University of California, Merced and, as a life-long learner, has studied Computer Science at Sacramento City College. Aubree combines her love for public service with a technical background to help grow the City’s innovation ecosystem to reach long-overlooked communities.
